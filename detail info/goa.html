Goa

Goa is a popular destination in India known for its sandy beaches, vibrant culture, and lively atmosphere. It offers a mix of scenic beauty and exciting nightlife, attracting tourists from around the world. Goa is famous for its Portuguese heritage, evident in its churches and architecture. Visitors enjoy water sports, delicious seafood, and the relaxed vibe, making it a perfect holiday spot.
